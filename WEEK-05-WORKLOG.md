### 3. Results Achieved

This week helped me understand how modern applications are packaged and deployed using Docker. I learned that Docker makes the application environment consistent and easier to move between local machines, EC2, and cloud services.

I also learned about Amazon ECR, which is useful for storing Docker images before deploying them to services such as ECS or other container platforms in the future.

The FCAJ Community Day event on May 23 was an important activity. Through the event, I gained more real-world understanding about AWS, AI, Serverless, and the working mindset of cloud engineers. It also helped me connect the theory I learned with practical use cases shared by speakers and the community.
