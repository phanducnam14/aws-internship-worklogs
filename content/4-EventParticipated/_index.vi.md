During the internship period, I had the opportunity to join several technology events that helped me expand my technical knowledge, gain practical insights from real business cases, and connect with the AWS community.

### Event 1

**Event Name:** AWS Vietnam Community Day 2026 (Saturday Meetup)

**Date & Time:** May 23, 2026 (09:00 - 12:00)

**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

**Role:** Attendee

**Event Description:**
This was a technical meetup where speakers from companies and teams such as GoTymeX, VPBank, and LotusHacks shared practical experiences in cloud system optimization, multi-agent AI architecture, CloudFront caching strategies, and challenges when applying LLMs in real production environments.

**Value Gained:**
Through this event, I gained a better understanding of CloudFront optimization techniques, including Regional Edge Cache, request collapsing, and the differences between CloudFront Functions and Lambda@Edge. Besides technical knowledge, I also learned more about contextual AI application design, MVP development mindset, teamwork, and how to handle pressure when building technology products.
